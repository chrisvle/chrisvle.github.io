Todd Morrill

Lecturer

Todd has taught at the Haas School of Business, University of California, Berkeley since 2001, where he is a Richard H. Holton Teaching Fellow and a Senior Fellow at the Lester Center for Entrepreneurship. He teaches Entrepreneurship, Lean Launchpad methodologies and other courses at Berkeley, UCSF, and at large and small companies. He has taught in the Intel Program in China and Japan, the Malaysia/UCSF program, and in programs in Estonia, Australia and Kuwait.

Experience

Todd has been building and leading companies since the first biotech boom in the early 1980s. He has worked a variety of VC-backed and large companies, and was a founder of three. He spent seven years in investment banking and has worked at multinational pharma, diagnostics and tools companies. His roles have included sales, product management, marketing, R&D, business development and CEO. He has been an independent Board member of two venture-backed companies.
