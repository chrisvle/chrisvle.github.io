Whitney Hischier

Specialization

Whitney Hischier specializes in helping organizations and individuals simplify complex problems. Through producing customized executive education and leadership programs, coaching and consulting, she leverages her network within UC Berkeley and across academia to bring together top thought leaders.

Experience

Whitney served as Assistant Dean of Executive Education at UC Berkeley for nearly a decade. During her tenure, she grew program revenues 8x and used her background in management consulting at Deloitte and KPMG to help clients build programs that addressed specific business and individual performance gaps.

Lecturer

Whitney is a lecturer at Haas in experiential and consulting-based courses including Problem Finding Problem Solving (MBA200P), Startup Lab (MBA295T), International Business Development (MBA298T), Managing and Motivating People (UG157.3), and Natural Leadership (equine guided education). She also oversees a number of student independent study projects in the entrepreneurship space.

Education

Whitney has a BA in International Relations with a focus on Security and Arms Control from Stanford University and an MBA with a Certificate in Healthcare Management from the Haas School of Business, UC Berkeley. She lives in Berkeley, CA with her husband, two children, two dogs, two cats and horse.
