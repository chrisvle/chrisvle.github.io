About Me

I come from an immigrant background and was born in Nha Trang, Vietnam. My family moved to California when I was about 3 years old. I come from my grandparents who collectively raised 22 children and survived one of the most terrifying wars in human history. As cliche as it sounds, my parents left everything they knew to go to a foreign land, where they did not know the language, struggled everyday against poverty and opportunistic landlords, and where my brother Matt and I had a chance to live lives of freedom. I come from a Christian household but for most of my 22 years I just took this as a childhood epithet. Through much research and reflection, I have found the Christian faith to be one grounded in history, rationality, and truth.
